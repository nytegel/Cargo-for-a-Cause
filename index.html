How it Works

We promote

Amerijet makes a 15 min presentation promoting CFC at your organization.

Members bring AWBs

Members ship & bring you’re their Airway Bills (AWB).

You send them to us

Your organization bring them to an Amerijet office.

We mail you a check

Amerijet sends your organization 5% of the AWB

What Next?

Amerijet will issue a check to your organization for 5 % of the airfreight of each Amerijet general prepaid shipment, not including accessorial charges, from all of the shipments that your members transport with us. Simply ask them to give you a copy of their air way bill (their shipment “receipt”) and send it to us with this form. We will mail a check to your organization for your philanthropic efforts. You can send as many unique Airway Bills as you’d like, as often as you’d like – there’s no limit to the amount that Amerijet will pay or you can earn!
